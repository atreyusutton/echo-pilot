## 7. Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
EchoPilot should sound:
- **Conversational** (like texting a friend)
- **Fast** (text is instant)
- **Helpful** (always ready to assist)
- **Simple** (no complicated instructions)
- **Human** (never robotic)

### Guidelines
- Write like you're texting  
- Short sentences (5-10 words)
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 per message max)
- Avoid technical jargon  
- Focus on speed and convenience
- Empathetic toward busy business owners  

### Approved Phrases
- "Just text back A to approve."  
- "Manage your business from your phone."  
- "Reply in seconds, not minutes."
- "We'll send you a text when you get a review."
- "Update your hours with a quick text."
- "No dashboard needed."

### Avoid
- "Synergistic automation solution."  
- "AI-powered platform"
- "Dashboard interface"
- "Advanced analytics"
- Anything that sounds like enterprise software

### Messaging Pillars
1. **SMS-first** â€“ Everything happens via text. Your phone is always with you.
2. **Speed** â€“ Respond in seconds while you're on the go. No logging in required.
3. **Simplicity** â€“ Reply with A, E, or S. Update hours with a text. That's it.
4. **Control** â€“ AI suggests, you decide. You're always in charge.
5. **Beyond reviews** â€“ Manage hours, posts, stats, review requestsâ€”all via text.
